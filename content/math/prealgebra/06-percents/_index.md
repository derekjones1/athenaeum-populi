When you deposit money in a savings account at a bank, it earns additional
money. Figuring out how your money will grow involves understanding and
applying concepts of percent. In this chapter, we will find out what
percents are and how we can use them to solve problems.

## Sections

- **Understand Percent** — using the definition of percent as a ratio out
  of 100, converting percents to fractions and decimals, and converting
  decimals and fractions to percents.
- **Solve General Applications of Percent** — translating and solving basic
  percent equations, and finding percent increase and decrease.
- **Solve Sales Tax, Commission, and Discount Applications** — computing
  sales tax and total cost, commission, discount and mark-up.
- **Solve Simple Interest Applications** — using the simple interest
  formula to find interest, principal, rate, or time.
- **Solve Proportions and their Applications** — writing and solving
  proportions, and using them to solve percent and similar-figure
  applications.
