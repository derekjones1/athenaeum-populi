This is the starting rung of the mathematics ladder: the foundations of
arithmetic and the first ideas of algebra. It assumes no prior coursework and
builds toward Algebra.

{{< callout type="info" >}}
  **Prerequisites:** none. This is a foundational book — a good place to begin.
{{< /callout >}}

## Chapters

This book is organized into chapters, each broken into short sections you can
work through in order.

- **Whole Numbers** — counting, place value, and the four operations.
- **The Language of Algebra** — variables, expressions, and how we write math.
