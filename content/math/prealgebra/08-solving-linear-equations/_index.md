  Solving linear equations using the subtraction, addition, division, and
  multiplication properties of equality, equations with variables and
  constants on both sides, and equations with fraction or decimal
  coefficients.
source_chapter: "8"

Teetering high above the floor, an amazing mobile remains aloft thanks to
its carefully balanced mass. Any shift in either direction could cause the
mobile to become lopsided, or even crash downward. In this chapter, we will
solve equations by keeping quantities on both sides of an equal sign in
perfect balance.

## Sections

- **Solve Equations Using the Subtraction and Addition Properties of
  Equality** — solving equations using the Subtraction and Addition
  Properties of Equality, solving equations that need to be simplified
  first, and translating word sentences and applications into equations to
  solve.
- **Solve Equations Using the Division and Multiplication Properties of
  Equality** — solving equations using the Division and Multiplication
  Properties of Equality, and solving equations that require simplifying
  first.
- **Solve Equations with Variables and Constants on Both Sides** — solving
  equations with constants, and with variables, on both sides.
- **Solve Equations with Fraction or Decimal Coefficients** — solving
  equations with fraction coefficients and with decimal coefficients.
