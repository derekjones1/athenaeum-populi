Whole numbers are the numbers we count with — 0, 1, 2, 3, and onward. This
chapter covers how they are named and written, and how to add, subtract,
multiply, and divide them with confidence.

## Sections

- **Introduction to Whole Numbers** — counting numbers, place value, naming
  and writing whole numbers, and rounding.
- **Add Whole Numbers** — the addition algorithm and its properties.
- **Subtract Whole Numbers** — the subtraction algorithm and borrowing.
- **Multiply Whole Numbers** — the multiplication algorithm and its
  properties.
- **Divide Whole Numbers** — the division algorithm, remainders, and
  applications.
